OUR SERVICES

JEWELLERY

We specialize in clicking the picture by keenly understanding the clients’ emotion topping it up with our craft. We believe in exhibiting things in simple yet elegant and minimalistic manner. An eye for detailing is what has helped us garner appreciation and accolades from our clients. Nothing misses either our lens, the eye behind the lens or the mind behind the eyes. Jewellery is the subject that needs minutest of the designs to be captured and we know exactly the way it needs to be treated.

FOOD

Vega creative studio communicates in graphic language and brings about the power of taste and scents through the lens of the camera. We bring alive the food items with the right combination of lighting, colours and background.

INDUSTRY

At Vega creative studio we lay tremendous focus on studying the exact needs of our clients and customizing the package as per the needs. Industry shoots is all about telling the tale of the machines and Vega weaves a story of a product that starts from raw material. This approach has helped us deliver an experience and not just a service.

PRODUCTS

Vega creative studio is the place where sensitivity meets sensibility, real life meets reel life, colours meet clicks, concepts meet campaigns and brands meet their audience. We not only master the art of showing the size, depth, texture and weight of the object but also expertise in styling, lighting and editing.

INTERIOR

Vega creative studio is a one stop solution for still photography or video shoots. Vega prides itself with a large portfolio of services ranging from inanimate photography to documentaries. Be it elegantly designed interiors or aesthetically appealing elevations of the heritage buildings Vega doesn’t miss the hidden beauty. The fine carvings on the wall, natural vegetable colours on the fresco painting or the colourful glasswork of the Mughal era Vega unfailingly captures the fine elements of the design.

Heritage

Weddings for Vega is an ultra-emotional affair and goes much beyond the realms of simply clicking and shooting the event. Weddings being once in a lifetime event gets super diligent treatment. From pre weddings to editing the entire journey is as special to Vega as the bride and grooms family. Selection of the themes, music and flavor of the entire project is highly personalized.
